	DATASET EXPLAINATIONS

The KR dataset (Kids Recode) contains information on children and their mothers, including variables related to child health, nutrition, and demographic characteristics. 

The Hr dataset (Household Recode) includes household-level data such as household characteristics, education, wealth index, and living conditions.
